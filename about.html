Hi, I'm Sakshi.

Designing where research, strategy, and empathy meet.

I'm fascinated by how good products can quietly shape the way we live, the kind of design that feels obvious once you see it, but only because someone put in the work to make it simple. That's what drew me into product design: the chance to take messy, complex problems and turn them into something people actually enjoy using.

What grounds my work is the conviction that the best decisions come from listening hard, to users, to data, and to people across the table with very different priorities. I'm at my best in cross-functional teams where empathy and analytical rigor pull in the same direction.
